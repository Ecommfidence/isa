Bardia Ghahremani, j.d.

Contact here

Bardia is the cornerstone of ISA. He has been successfully representing NFL, MMA and motorsports athletes since 2001. He graduated from UC Santa Barbara and the Claude W. Pettit College of Law at Ohio Northern University. His distinctive ability to connect with clients and industry professionals alike has made him one of the most respected player representatives in the business.

As the Chief Executive Officer, Bardia ensures that every client with ISA is provided with a full-service client-agent relationship that reaches even beyond the extent of his professional obligations. Bardia and his team have developed a precise formula using market analysis and promotional techniques that help place ISA clients into successful career opportunities at the next level via the NFL Draft and free agency.

Most importantly, with an ever changing economic climate, Bardia has been able to consistently devise and negotiate creative player contracts that provide financial stability and roster leverage for his clients.

When it comes to charity and community involvement, Bardia is also is an active board member for the I.S.A. Children’s Foundation. This organization was proudly created to help link ISA clients, staff and community members to various children’s and humanitarian causes throughout the world.

In 2014, Bardia was the recipient of the esteemed “40 Under 40” honor by OC Metro Magazine as one the forty most successful individuals under the age of 40 in Orange County, California.

Bardia currently resides in Mission Viejo, California.
