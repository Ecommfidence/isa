David Lee

David Lee was named President of ISA Basketball in 2016. David is a certified NBA and FIBA agent. Prior to ISA, David worked as a basketball consultant and recruiter at Rival Sports Group where he gained a tremendous wealth of experience in the day-to-day management of NBA talent. David worked closely with players like Avery Bradley, Tony Snell, Jordan Farmar and Josh Heustis.

Originally, David began his career in basketball as collegiate assistant coach. From 2003 to 2013 Davidâ€™s coaching stops included UC Irvine, Grand Canyon University and Mesa Community College (AZ). In 2013, David decided to make a career change into the sports management field. Becoming an agent made total sense. It was a way remain involved with the game he loves while still helping college players make a transition to the next level. David has quickly evolved as an agent and has developed a very strong network both within NBA and FIBA organizations to help serve his clients best interests.

David has a B.A. in Psychology from the University of Arizona and also a B.A. in Business Communication from Arizona State University. David currently resides in Playa Del Rey, CA with his wife Julia.
